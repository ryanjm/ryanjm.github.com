Matt pointed me to a great slide share called 26 Time Management Hacks I Wish I'd Known at 20. I think it is a great list of reminders and some of them really stood out to me.

  • Work more when you're in the zone. Relax when you're not.
  • Work iteratively.
  • Put meetings at the beginning of the day.
  • Keep the same context throughout the day. Switching between projects/clients is unproductive.
  • Always take notes.
