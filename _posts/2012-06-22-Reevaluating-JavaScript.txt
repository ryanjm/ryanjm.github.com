**Arrays vs lists**  
For those who don't know, this is a simple CS issue. Short version: lists are better. As simple as possible: memory is like a rows of buckets, each with their own address. Array's are usually implemented such that you are grabbing a row of buckets, based on their address. If you want the first object, you like at your beginning address.
